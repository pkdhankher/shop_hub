Data Deletion Request

Shop Hub is a shopping aggregator app that opens third-party shopping websites using WebView. We do not collect or store any personal data from our users on our own servers. However, we are committed to transparency and respect your privacy rights.

Need to Request Data Deletion?

If you believe your data has been shared unintentionally, or you have interacted with our app and want to request deletion of any associated data (e.g., analytics, crash logs, or emails), please contact us directly.

Contact Us

Email us with the subject line "Data Deletion Request" at:

assist.shophub@gmail.com

Please include any relevant details such as:

  • Your Google account email (if applicable)
  • Date of usage
  • Device details (optional)

We will respond to your request within 7 business days and take necessary steps if applicable.
